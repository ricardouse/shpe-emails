Dear SHPE Professionals and Alumni,
We hope your fall is going as good as ours! SHPE-UH has really had an amazing run this semester. Students have had the chance of touring facilities, meeting and sharing with Professional SHPEtinas, and volunteering with high schoolers as well as dogs!
Things don't stop there however! In celebration of Hispanic Heritage Month, a few of our members have been featured by the Cullen College of Engineering. Read more about some of them in the previous links!
All in all, we're doing big things.

Fifth General Meeting
Dow Chemical will be coming to our fifth general meeting of the semester to interface with students and share industry advice. This will be taking place on October 25th, 7PM to 9PM at the College of Social Work. Come see us and learn more about the oingoing activities at SHPE-UH.

GMiS HENAAC Conference
A number of our members will be heading out to LA for HENAAC 2018. We'll be having a pre-conference workshop where SHPEsters that have been to HENAAC or similar conferences will share tips with those attending this year. Preparation is key!

Professional Mentor Coffee Chat
The first Coffee Chat of the semester will be on 10/16/2018 at 7PM. We'll be meeting with a few professionals at The Nook to talk about work, school and life in general. At this event professionals are not coming representing their companies, but themselves!
Please consider joining this program. You could change the lives of students for the best!
