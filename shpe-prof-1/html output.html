Dear SHPE Professionals and Alumni,
My name is Ricardo Useche. I am a Computer Science student at the University of Houston and will be serving as our Chapter's Professional Chair for the 2018-2019 year.
As such, I hope to form meaningful relationships with all of you and continue to get your support in promoting the growth of our members. At the end of the day, it is people like you who have built the path and set the examples we follow.
We're excited to start executing on the plans made during the Summer and want to thank you for your continued support. Please take this email as a cordial invitation to all our events this semester.
Additionally, we'd like to once more congratulate those that have gone from students to professionals, making the transition that all of us aspire to make!

Professional Mentor Program
As you may know, we have had a pretty successful MentorSHPE program where upperclassmen provide support for other SHPE-UH members. Be it helping with a tough class or having someone to rely on during rough times, mentors and mentees have worked together to achieve success in a variety of ways.
We are taking this further by having Alumni/Professionals take on the mentor role. The goal of the program is simple: connect with our students and share the type of knowledge you only gain outside of school.
We have tentatively selected two dates to hold Professional Mentor Socials: Tuesday, Oct 16 and Friday, Nov 16.
Our hope is to have a few Professionals join this initiative and then announce this program at the 1st General Meeting. We will then open applications for students and pick a small subset of quality candidates to match with professionals. Join below!
